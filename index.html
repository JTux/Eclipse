About us

In the early 2000s, three friends – Maya, Alex, and Daniel – came together to start a company that would change the world. They were all passionate about space exploration and renewable energy, and saw an opportunity to create something truly innovative by combining their interests.

Maya was a brilliant scientist with a PhD in physics, specializing in renewable energy technologies. Alex was an engineer with a background in aerospace, and had worked on several projects related to space exploration. And Daniel was a business strategist with a passion for sustainability and social responsibility.

Together, the three of them spent months working out of Maya's garage, experimenting with different designs and technologies for solar panels and wind turbines. They faced many challenges along the way, but they remained committed to their vision of creating a new kind of energy company that would be both sustainable and profitable.

As they began to gain traction, Maya, Alex, and Daniel attracted the attention of investors who saw the potential of their vision. With the new funding, they were able to hire more engineers and scientists, and expand their operations.

Today, Eclipse is a leading renewable energy company, with a reputation for innovation and quality. The founders of the company remain involved, providing guidance and inspiration to the new generation of engineers and scientists who are carrying on their vision of a more sustainable and energy-efficient world.

Branding

Brand Overview

Eclipse is a tech company focused on renewable energy, technology, and space exploration. Our brand's color scheme reflects our celestial and sustainable energy focus, with dark blue, dark gray, gold, green, orange, purple, and white colors used to create a sophisticated, modern, and impactful color scheme. Our logo features the eclipse symbol, representing both our brand name and celestial theme. We strive to be innovative and creative in all aspects of our business and are committed to advancing renewable energy technology for a sustainable future.

Our Logo

Eclipse uses a logo that depicts the sun partially covered by the moon during a solar eclipse. This logo reflects the company's focus on renewable energy and space exploration.

Colors

Eclipse's color scheme combines dark blue, dark gray, gold, green, orange, purple, and white colors to create a sophisticated and impactful design. Dark blue and dark gray are the primary colors used in Eclipse's color scheme, representing the vast expanse of space and the mystery and wonder of the universe. Gold and orange are used as accent colors, adding warmth and energy to the design and representing the brand's focus on renewable energy. Green is used to emphasize sustainability, while purple represents creativity and innovation. White is used for text and to create a clean and modern look. By combining these colors, we create a sophisticated, modern, and impactful color scheme that reflects our brand's focus on renewable energy, technology, and space exploration.
